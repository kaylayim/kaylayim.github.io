About Us

Businesses will rise and fall but the people are the ones who make anything worth pursuing. Community is at the core of everything we do at Kayla's Cafe. Without our dedicated customers and dynamic staff, we wouldn't be where we are today. Kayla's Cafe is more than just specialty pastries and drinks. We’re more than our focus on quality roasting and sourcing practices. Kayla's Cafe is our commitment to providing a safe, inviting space to all who walk through our doors. From hosting monthly events, to showcasing local artists, we take pride in providing a sanctuary for all coffee lovers to enjoy.
