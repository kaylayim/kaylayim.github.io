Introduction

KAY

KAY is a small business that focuses on selling quality streetwear apparel such as hoodies, t-shirts, jackets, and hats that anyone can incorporate into their outfits. The shop opened in 2020 due to the founder’s passion for fashion and mission to make everyone feel confident when they wear KAY’s merch.

Target audience

KAY’s target audiences are young adults and Gen-Z who love to experiment with fashion or want something casual to wear. These groups want to discover brands that share their values and wear a logo that represents who they are. They are still exploring their fashion style and KAY’s streetwear allows them to reach their creative vision.

Due to the pandemic, consumers have switched over to online shopping so they would be visiting the site to see the newest streetwear drops and complete their purchases there. The website will be designed to give customers a seamless shopping experience so that they can focus on finding the perfect outfit for them.

About Us

KAY was first established in 2020 by Kayla Yim who had a passion for streetwear and strived to make anyone feel as good as they look. KAY is a small business that focuses on selling quality streetwear apparel such as hoodies, t-shirt, jackets, and hats that anyone can incorporate into their outfits. Clothes are a form of self-expression and Kayla hopes to encourage people to assert their style and individuality through her products.
